Wildfire on screen.
Who's talking about it?

2.8% of 37,453 scripts mention
any climate keyword at all
— Lear Center, 2022

Only 0.6% of scripted TV and film mentioned "climate change" between 2016 and 2020. Wildfire barely registers at all.

This research asks: when films do depict wildfire, how do they talk about it? What frames, whose voices, what emotional register?
