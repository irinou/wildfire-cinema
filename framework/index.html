Cinematic Representation of the Climate Crisis and Wildfires

Wildfire on screen.
Who is talking about it?

2.8% of 37,453 scripts mention
any climate keyword at all
LEAR CENTER, 2022

Only 0.6% of scripted TV and film mentioned "climate change" between 2016 and 2020. Wildfire barely registers.

This research asks: when films do show wildfire, how do they talk about it? What frames, whose voices, what emotional register?
